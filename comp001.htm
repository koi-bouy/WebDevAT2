Minerals mined in WA

  • Most of Rio Tinto's WA Pilbara Mines extract Iron Ore.
  • Salt is mined at their Dampier location
  • Spodumene (used to produce Lithium) is mined at their Mt Cattlin location
  • Copper is mined at their Winu location
